About S. K. Lahoti Associates

The firm was established in Secunderabad in 1989 and is engaged in the practice of accountancy.

The firm is constituted as a partnership of Chartered Accountants in practice, supported by a team of professionals and staff. It provides services in the areas of audit and assurance, taxation, accounting, regulatory and exchange-control matters, and corporate advisory.

Further information about the firm and its partners is set out on the About and Team pages.
