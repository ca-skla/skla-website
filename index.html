Foreign investors & NRIs
Across borders

Entering India, or managing it from abroad?

We help foreign companies set up and stay compliant in India, and we help NRIs and OCIs manage tax, property and remittances back home — with the RBI and tax certifications taken care of.

  • India entry done right

    The right entity, incorporated and operational the first time.

  • Exchange-control clean

    FC-GPR, FC-TRS, FLA and ODI filings, accurate and on time.

  • Repatriation handled

    15CA / 15CB (now Form 145 / 146) certification and lower-TDS certificates for non-residents.
