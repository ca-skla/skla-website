About the Firm

S. K. Lahoti Associates is a firm of Chartered Accountants in practice, established in 1989 at Secunderabad and presently constituted as a partnership.

Our Story

The firm

The firm was founded by CA. Sunil Kumar Lahoti at Hyderabad in 1989.

The firm is engaged in the practice of accountancy and provides services in the areas of audit and assurance, taxation, accounting, regulatory and exchange-control matters, and corporate advisory.

The firm advises clients in India and, in cross-border matters, clients situated outside India, including non-residents.
