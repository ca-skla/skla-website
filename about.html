About the firm

A firm of Chartered Accountants in practice, established in 1989 at Secunderabad and today constituted as a partnership.

Our story

Thirty-five years of steady judgment.

The firm was founded by CA. Sunil Kumar Lahoti at Hyderabad in 1989.

We are engaged in the practice of accountancy, providing services across audit and assurance, taxation, accounting, regulatory and exchange-control matters, and corporate advisory.

We advise clients in India and, in cross-border matters, clients situated outside India — including foreign investors and non-residents.
