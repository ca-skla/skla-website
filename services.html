Services

Six areas of practice — spanning audit and assurance, taxation, regulatory and exchange-control matters, accounting, and corporate advisory. The descriptions below are provided for information.

01 · Assurance

Audit & Assurance

Audit and assurance engagements under the applicable statutes and standards.

We audit your financial statements under the Companies Act and other laws, and give a clear, independent opinion stakeholders can rely on.

  • Companies Act audits
  • LLP & firm audits
  • Trust & society audits
  • Reporting to stakeholders

Audit and reporting under Section 44AB, handled cleanly and filed on time — so a routine requirement never becomes a problem.

  • Form 3CA / 3CB & 3CD
  • Turnover & eligibility review
  • Disallowance analysis
  • Timely e-filing

We review how your business actually runs, find where money and time leak out, and help you tighten up before you scale.

  • Internal control evaluation
  • Risk-based audit plans
  • SOP design & review
  • Management reporting

Independent checks for lenders, buyers and management who need to trust the numbers before they act.

  • Stock & debtor audits
  • Bank / concurrent audits
  • Financial due diligence
  • Investigation & forensic support
02 · Taxation

Direct, Indirect & International Tax

Services across direct, indirect and international taxation.

Planning and filing for individuals, firms and companies — built around your real position, not a template.

  • Corporate & personal ITR filing
  • Advance tax & TDS compliance
  • Tax planning & structuring
  • Capital gains advisory

The full GST cycle — registration, returns, credits and refunds — kept accurate and current.

  • GST registration & returns
  • Input tax credit reconciliation
  • GST refunds & exports / LUT
  • Annual return & GST audit

Cross-border tax advice for multinationals and global founders, built on the arm’s-length principle and treaty benefits.

  • Transfer pricing study & documentation
  • DTAA & treaty benefit advisory
  • Cross-border structuring
  • Form 3CEB & TP compliance

If a notice or assessment lands, we stand in front of it — representing you before authorities and appellate forums.

  • Assessment & scrutiny support
  • Appeals & rectifications
  • Notices & representation
  • Refund follow-up
03 · Regulatory

India Entry, FEMA & FDI

Services relating to India entry, FEMA and FDI for foreign investors.

We help you pick the right vehicle and get you incorporated and operational — properly, the first time.

  • Private limited & subsidiary setup
  • Branch, liaison & project offices
  • Wholly-owned subsidiary structuring
  • PAN, TAN, bank account & GST setup

Exchange-control reporting that’s accurate and on time, so your investment stays clean in the eyes of the RBI.

  • Form FC-GPR (share issue to non-residents)
  • Form FC-TRS (transfer of shares)
  • Annual FLA return to RBI
  • ODI / overseas investment reporting

We confirm eligibility, sector caps and route up front — and secure approvals where they’re needed.

  • FDI eligibility & sectoral cap review
  • Automatic vs. approval route advisory
  • Government / RBI approvals
  • KYC & AML coordination via AD banks

RBI-compliant pricing and valuation when shares or instruments are issued or transferred to non-residents.

  • Valuation of shares & instruments
  • Pricing-guideline compliance
  • Fair-value certification
  • Coordination with merchant bankers
04 · Cross-border

NRI & Expatriate Services

Services to non-residents in relation to Indian taxation, property and remittances.

We get your residency right and file accurately — claiming every relief and benefit you’re entitled to.

  • NRI / OCI income tax returns
  • Residential status determination
  • Exemptions & deductions
  • DTAA relief & foreign tax credit

Stop paying excess tax on Indian income and property sales with the right Section 197 certificate.

  • Lower / NIL deduction certificates
  • TDS on property advisory
  • Application & AO liaison
  • Refund optimisation

Buying, holding or selling Indian property as a non-resident — handled end to end, with the tax planned for.

  • Capital gains computation
  • Reinvestment / exemption planning
  • TDS compliance on sale
  • Documentation & advisory

Move funds out of India smoothly and compliantly, with the certifications banks ask for.

  • Form 15CA & 15CB certification
  • Repatriation from NRO / NRE
  • Remittance documentation
  • Bank & FEMA coordination
05 · Operations

Accounting & Outsourcing

Accounting and outsourcing support, so your books and compliance stay current.

Clean, current books on modern accounting platforms — ready whenever you need them.

  • Day-to-day bookkeeping
  • Financial statement compilation
  • Ledger scrutiny & reconciliation
  • Cloud accounting setup

Payroll run end to end, with every statutory deduction and filing taken care of.

  • Payroll processing
  • PF, ESI & PT compliance
  • TDS on salaries
  • Compliance calendar management

Senior financial leadership when you need it — the insight of a CFO without the full-time cost.

  • Management reporting & MIS
  • Budgeting & cash-flow planning
  • Financial analysis & KPIs
  • Board / investor reporting support
06 · Advisory

Corporate Law & Business Advisory

Corporate law and business advisory services across the company lifecycle.

Stay on the right side of the Companies Act and MCA, year after year, without the worry.

  • Company & LLP incorporation
  • Annual ROC filings
  • Director & share-capital changes
  • Statutory registers & minutes

From idea to incorporation to your first audit — one partner for the whole journey.

  • Entity selection & registration
  • Startup India & DPIIT recognition
  • Founder & ESOP structuring
  • Investor-readiness & compliance

Independent valuations and the certificates banks, regulators and investors ask for.

  • Business & share valuations
  • Net-worth & turnover certificates
  • Statutory certifications
  • Form 15CA / 15CB

Bankable project reports and financials that help get your funding approved.

  • Project reports for loan approval
  • Projected financial statements
  • CMA data preparation
  • Bank liaison & documentation
